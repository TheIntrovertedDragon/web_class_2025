First, some background knowledge:

Wings of Fire is a fantasy book series, marketed for 8-11 year olds. It is written by Tui T. Sutherland, who is also the author of the Menagerie trilogy, the Spirit Animal series, and more.

Here is her website:

tuibooks.com
