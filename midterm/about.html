Summary:

Wings of Fire is a book series about a world of dragons. There are two continents: Pyrrhia and Pantala. Seven tribes of dragons live on Pyrrhia: Mudwings, Skywing, Sandwings, Seawings, Rainwings, Icewings, and Nightwings. Meanwhile, three tribes live on Pantala: Silkwings, Hivewings, and Leafwings.

The tribes' traits and special abilities:

Mudwings: Thick, armored, brown scales, with amber undertones. Very strong. Can breath fire if warm enough. Hold breath for an hour, and blend into mud puddles; flat heads with nostrils on top. Eye color from amber to brown to gold. Those born from blood red eggs will instantly heal after being burned, so they're basically immune to fire.

Queen: Queen Moorhen

Skywings: Red or orange scales, with gold details. Long necks, yellow eyes. Fastest fliers, with the largest wings. Breath fire.

Queen: Queen Ruby

Sandwings: Pale yellow/tan scales, like desert sand. Forked black tongues and black eyes. Can survive in intense heat and almost no water or food. Poisonous scorpion barb at the end of tail.

Queen: Queen Thorn

Seawings: Blue/green scales and eyes. Can breathe underwater, due to the gills on their necks. Glow in the dark scales. Can see in the dark. Powerful swimmers, with strong tails, and webbed talons.

Queen: Queen Coral

Rainwings: Color-changing scales based on their emotions, so they come in all sorts of colors, a frill behind their ears, and prehensile tails. They are vegetarians, and can get energy from sunlight. Can camoflauge into the background. Spit a deadly, acidic venom from they fangs.

Queen: Queen Glory

Icewings: Shimmering white-blue scales and blue eyes, with spikes on the back of head and end of whip-thin tail. Withstand sub-zero temperatures. Breathe a deadly frostbreath.

Queen: Queen Snowfall

Nightwings: Dark-black scales, with with white scales under the wings, like a night sky full of stars. Forked black tongues. Can breath fire. If born under one full moon, the Nightwing either has mindreading or futuresight. Mindreaders have silver teardrop scales next to their eyes. Under two full moons, the Nightwing has mindreading and futuresight. Under three, the powers are much stronger.

Queen: Queen Glory

Silkwings: Scales in all colors, except black. Vegetarians. Two antennae that can sense air vibrations. Four butterfly-like wings that grow out at age 6, after a Metamorphasis, where the Silkwing wraps itself in silk for a few days to grow wings. During Metamorphasis and after, Silkwings can shoot silk from their wrists; some rare Silkwings have silk that can burn, called flame silk.

Former Queen: Queen Monarch

Hivewings: Yellow/orange/red and black scales. Four wings like a dragonfly. Some dragons have natural weapons, like a stinger at the end of the tail, stingers on wrists, poisonous fangs, paralyzing nerve toxins, etc.

Queen: Queen Jewel

Leafwings: Green scales, possibly with gold undertones. Leaf-shaped wings. Some have the power to control plants and make them grow abnormally and quickly.

Queen: Queen Sequoia

Scavengers: Believe it or not, there are humans in this world. They are considered prey, though some dragons are starting to become friends with them, due to their intelligence. They enjoy stealing treasure from dragons.

I don't want to spoil the stories, but basically, the first five books is about a 20 year long war, and 5 dragonets destined to end it. The second five are about the legend of Darkstalker, with many adventures of power and animus magic. The third five books are about the new continent, Pantala, where Silkwings are the servants of Hivewings and the Leafwings are supposedly wiped out. The side books add more information and depth to the story.
